About MARC GameLab

Our Story

MARC GameLab was founded in 2024 with a passion for gaming and a vision to create a premier gaming destination. Our mission is to provide an unparalleled gaming experience, where gamers can connect, compete, and enjoy their favorite titles in a comfortable environment.

Why MARC GameLab?

As a sub-brand of the MARC group, we are committed to excellence. Customers choose us for our:

  • Latest gaming consoles and accessories.
  • Welcoming and social atmosphere.
  • Affordable rates and flexible booking options.
  • Friendly and knowledgeable staff ready to assist you.

Location & Timings

We are located in the heart of the city, easily accessible for all gaming enthusiasts. Our opening hours are:

  • Monday to Friday: 10 AM - 11 PM
  • Saturday and Sunday: 10 AM - 12 AM

Our Goals

Our primary goals include:

  • Providing a safe and inclusive space for gamers of all ages.
  • Encouraging social interaction and community building through gaming.
  • Regularly hosting tournaments and events to engage our customers.
